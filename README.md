# Energy Conservation Interactive

Interactive HTML visualization of the conservation of energy.

This small browser-based project helps beginners see how potential energy, kinetic energy, and thermal energy change over time while total energy stays conserved.

## Open the interactive demo

Open `energy_conservation_visualizer.html` in a browser.

No installation is required.

## What you can explore

- Mass
- Gravity
- Starting height
- Air drag
- Bounce loss
- Playback speed

The interactive view shows how:
- blue = potential energy
- gold = kinetic energy
- red = thermal energy produced by dissipation
